Research Defence: Breast Cancer Classification

This study leveraged the Wisconsin Diagnostic Breast Cancer Dataset (WDBC) to predict breast cancer malignancy using five machine learning classifiers: Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, Random Forest, K-Nearest Neighbors, and Naive Bayes.

  • Best Model: K-Nearest Neighbors (98.83% accuracy).
  • Employed hyperparameter tuning to optimize model performance.
  • Highlighted the significance of early cancer detection through ML techniques.
